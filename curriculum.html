NEMPS offers a balanced curriculum that integrates academics with co-curricular and extracurricular activities, ensuring the all-round development of every student.

Primary (I–V)

Activity-based, foundational learning that builds strong literacy and numeracy.

Middle (VI–VIII)

Concept-driven learning across languages, mathematics, science and social science.

Secondary (IX–X)

Rigorous CBSE-aligned preparation with continuous assessment and skill building.

Languages

English medium instruction with additional language options.

STEM & Labs

Hands-on science and computer practicals to make learning tangible.

Co-Curricular

Arts, sports and clubs that nurture creativity and leadership.
