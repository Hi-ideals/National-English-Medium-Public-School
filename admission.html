How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Enquiry

Submit the enquiry form or visit the school office to express interest.

2

Application

Collect and complete the application form with required details.

3

Interaction

A brief interaction with the student and parents.

4

Confirmation

Complete admission formalities and fee payment to confirm the seat.

Documents Required

  • ✓ Birth Certificate of the student
  • ✓ Transfer Certificate (for classes other than entry level)
  • ✓ Report card / marksheet of the previous class
  • ✓ Passport-size photographs
  • ✓ Aadhaar card of the student
  • ✓ Address and ID proof of parent/guardian

For detailed eligibility and the current fee schedule, please see the Fee Structure or contact the school office at +91 9482429210.
