National English Medium Public School (NEMPS) is a prestigious institution established in 2016 within the B.V.B. Campus in Bidar District.

Founded under the aegis of the Hyderabad Karnataka Education Society (HKES), the school upholds the rich legacy of its parent organization, which was initiated in 1958 by the visionary Shri Mahadevappa Rampure. HKES was created to meet the rising demand for higher education in diverse fields such as medicine, engineering, pharmacy, law, and other technical and skilled areas.

NEMPS was conceptualized to provide a platform for quality education that aligns with the society's broader vision of fostering academic excellence and holistic development. Since its inception, the school has achieved remarkable milestones, growing steadily while maintaining its core values of nurturing intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical behavior.

The institution offers a balanced curriculum that integrates academics with co-curricular and extracurricular activities, ensuring the all-round development of its students. It emphasizes creating a nurturing environment that inspires students to become responsible, innovative, and socially conscious citizens capable of contributing positively to society.
