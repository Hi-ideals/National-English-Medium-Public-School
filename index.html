About Our School

A prestigious institution established in 2016

National English Medium Public School (NEMPS) is a prestigious institution established in 2016 within the B.V.B. Campus in Bidar District. Founded under the aegis of the Hyderabad Karnataka Education Society (HKES), the school upholds the rich legacy of its parent organization, initiated in 1958 by the visionary Shri Mahadevappa Rampure.

Since its inception, the school has achieved remarkable milestones, growing steadily while maintaining its core values of nurturing intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical behavior — ensuring the all-round development of its students.

Principal's Message

Nurturing young minds, shaping the future

It is with immense pride and joy that I welcome you to National English Medium Public School, an institution dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping the future of our society. Since its establishment in 2016, our school has stood as a beacon of educational excellence, inspired by the rich legacy of the Hyderabad Karnataka Education Society.

At NEMPS, we believe that education is not merely about academics but also about instilling values, fostering creativity, and nurturing the potential of every child. Through our modern infrastructure, co-curricular activities, and community-focused initiatives, we aim to develop well-rounded individuals equipped with the skills and confidence to face the challenges of tomorrow.
