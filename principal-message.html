Mrs. Srilatha G. Swamy

Principal, NEMPS

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers,

It is with immense pride and joy that I welcome you to National English Medium Public School, an institution dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping the future of our society. Since its establishment in 2016, our school has stood as a beacon of educational excellence, inspired by the rich legacy of the Hyderabad Karnataka Education Society.

At NEMPS, we believe that education is not merely about academics but also about instilling values, fostering creativity, and nurturing the potential of every child. Our mission is to provide a holistic learning environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical responsibility. We strive to create an atmosphere where students feel empowered to dream big, work hard, and contribute positively to society.

Our dedicated team of educators is committed to delivering quality education while ensuring that each student is supported in their journey of growth and discovery. Through our modern infrastructure, co-curricular activities, and community-focused initiatives, we aim to develop well-rounded individuals equipped with the skills and confidence to face the challenges of tomorrow.

As we continue to grow, we remain steadfast in our vision to empower students to excel academically, socially, and emotionally. I invite you to join us on this transformative journey, where together, we can shape a brighter future for our children and society at large.

Warm regards,
Mrs. Srilatha G. Swamy
Principal, National English Medium Public School
