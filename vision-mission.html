Our Vision

Transforming potential into excellence

To transform potential into excellence and empower students to shape a brighter future for themselves and the world — developing responsible, innovative and socially conscious citizens.

Our Mission

Holistic, value-based education

NEMPS is committed to imparting high quality holistic education by giving students the opportunities to develop their creative and social skills through a wide variety of programmes in a caring, innovative and healthy environment.

What We Stand For

Academic Excellence

A balanced, rigorous curriculum that prepares students for the future.

Creativity

Co-curricular activities that enhance creativity and leadership skills.

Values

Instilling ethics, empathy and social responsibility.

Community

Community-oriented initiatives that give back to society.
